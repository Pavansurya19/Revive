About Revive

Revive is India's pioneering rent–swap–buy platform dedicated to revolutionizing ethical fashion. We believe fashion should empower people and protect the planet, not harm them.

Our mission is to make sustainable fashion truly affordable, circular, and transparent—accessible to every conscious Indian wardrobe.

We collaborate closely with local artisans, organic cotton farmers, and certified ethical manufacturers to craft garments that are beautiful, durable, and kind to the Earth. By connecting these makers directly with consumers through rent, swap, and purchase options, we reduce waste and uplift communities.

At Revive, you don’t just wear clothes; you share stories, support traditions, and join a growing movement redefining India’s fashion future. Our platform makes it easy to choose clothing that aligns with your values—without compromising style or affordability. Whether it’s the convenience of renting elegant pieces, the fun of swapping loved garments, or the pride in buying artisan-crafted clothes, every choice contributes to a healthier planet and fairer economy.

Together, we imagine a world where every garment tells a story of empowerment, sustainability, and hope.

Our Environmental & Social Impact

At Revive, impact isn’t just a value—it’s a continuous, transparent result of every action our community takes. We’re committed to solving fashion’s waste problem and supporting the people behind each garment.

Environmental Impact:

  • For every 1,000 garments circulated, we prevent over 3,000 kg of CO₂ emissions—that’s equivalent to planting 75 mature trees.
  • We save more than 1.5 million liters of water—protecting a vital natural resource in a water-stressed country.
  • More than 500 kg of textile waste are diverted from landfill and incineration, drastically reducing fashion’s environmental footprint.
Our rent–swap–buy model directly challenges the take-make-waste cycle of fast fashion, giving new life to every piece.

Social Empowerment:

  • By buying, renting, and swapping with Revive, you uplift skilled Indian artisans and makers through fair wages and long-term partnerships.
  • We work to preserve time-honored crafts—from embroidery to weaving—by ensuring artisans can thrive in the modern economy.
  • Our transparent supply chain upholds safe working conditions at every step, so the clothes you wear are as ethical as they are elegant.
Community events, campus drives, and digital engagement help seed a new culture of conscious consumption across India.

With every choice on Revive, you become a changemaker.
Every garment counts. Every person matters.
